This is some of my in-design work
This piece is the contents page to a magazine I created using InDesign. The magazine was based around the idea of Playstation 5 vs Xbox One X. The idea for this contents page is that all the platforms for holding the page numbers and page names are all branching of from the Xbox One X. This works well because of the box shaped design of the Xbox. This was the page dedicated to the PlayStation 5. I used the PlayStation blue colours for the design of the background and then found the font used by Sony for there name and also found a font simialar to the PlayStation font, for the main heading. I wanted to show the PlayStation itself and also the new remote, so I found an image online, did some editing using Adobe Photoshop and then brought both iamges into InDesign. This page was dedicated to the most popular characters within the Xbox universe. I sourced information about these characters online and then added them to the document. I choose to use three characters and then have a paragraph talking about them and there roles within their games. I then took an image of one of these characters, did some slight editing to it and placed it on the page. To give it an imteresting look, I made the text form around the character.
